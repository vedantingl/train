Tips for a Great Train Journey

Plan Ahead

Book your tickets early, check the train schedule, and arrive at the station with time to spare.

Pack Smart

Bring essentials like snacks, a water bottle, a book, or headphones for entertainment.

Stay Comfortable

Wear comfy clothes, bring a neck pillow, and keep a light blanket for long journeys.

Enjoy the Scenery

Choose a window seat and take in the beautiful landscapes as you travel.
